ABOUT

My name is Tino Machida and welcome to my Git- Page portfolio. I am a recent graduate Computer Science Major (Software Engineering Concerntration) from the University of Manitoba. My program was very challenging but still just mentally stimulating enough to keep me interested in what I was doing. At times I would work on a problem and encounter brick walls and feel like a solution was not viable. During my first years I had no idea what Computer Science was all about, (very evident with me feeling stuck and just giving up) but as dove deeper into my program I realized that it's all about solving problems and bottlenecks. The program tested every aspect of my resolve to complete it and through it I became a more resilient thinker.

The process involved a great deal of independent research, requiring initiative, self-motivation and learning a wide range of skills and technologies. It consisted of courses like Database Systems, Analysis of Algorithms, Web Technologies, Software Engineering, Networking, Specification and design of Graphical User Interface, Distributed Systems, Technical Communication and a whole load of Mathematics..lol The program assisted me to gain basic but essential knowledge in the multiple fields of Computer Science.

Adapting to various work cultures, identifying and resolving bottlenecks and prioritizing have been striking features of my working style. In addition to the above, I strongly believe in learning and sharing my knowledge with my colleagues. “Knowledge parted is knowledge gained” has been my motto in life. I am always keen to learn and am sharp at identifying and solving problems

When I am not stuck typing away code behind a screen you can find me playing around with various digital audio workstations and producing music or in a park somewhere enjoying the outdoors.

Languages

It was uphill, a challenge for me and I struggled a lot to learn the basic syntax as I had never programmed anything in my whole entire life. I painstakingly learned the syntax and libraries from the ground up. It was a slow process but soon enough I was actually writing code. I eventually became good at writing sensible python code it but before I knew it I ran into new hurdles.

Projects

Complete
AirDnD

This was a Software Engineering 2 class project whereby we had to build a fully working mobile and Web app, linked to the same server. I teamed up with 8 other students to deliver a minimum viable, fully functional app. The app is pretty much a dungeons and dragons character customization suite, with a database/ login and character update properties. An EC-2 instance with node.js was used to implement the database together with SQL. For the front end we used React and React-native for the purposes of time saving. React-native was chose for it's build once, deploy everywhere feature which allows for both I-OS and Android apps so share the same code base.


Politics Gallery

This was a me just clearly having fun and demonstrating my knowledge of computer graphics and physics. This is a simple script built in processing (P3D). Initially it was a Graphics 1 project which was meant to simulate a simple 3D world with different camera angles and also allowing for movement and camera movement. I am guessing one bored Saturday I got bored and got too carried away and turned it into an entire politics gallery. The gallery features some prominent figures in politics today like Trump, Trudeau, Obama, Clinton, you know, the regulars. It allows for a first person movement and 3 different camera angles.


HowFit 1

I had the covid isolation blues and decided to brush up on some front end languages which i hadn't touched in a while. This is a simple Hack+ PHP + Javascript and HTML app that allows for the user to enter in their bio-metrics and get their BMR and BMI. Not much work was put in here.
